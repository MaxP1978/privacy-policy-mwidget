Privacy Policy

for "Mwidgets pro"

Published by: Dew MaxP

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. All features are available without registration and do not access sensitive information.

The app requests system permissions solely to display status data through widgets. These include access to:

  • Device battery status
  • Connected Bluetooth device charge
  • Step count and activity tracking
  • RAM and internal storage information
  • Weather data and location (if enabled)

No data is transmitted to third parties. All processing is done locally on the device. The app does not use advertising or third-party analytics.
